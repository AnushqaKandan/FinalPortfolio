About Hi, I'm Anushqa

Empowering Change: I Lead with Heart and Vision!

My passion for coding ignited when I first joined the tech team at my church. I remember the excitement of being entrusted with setting up sound systems, handling projectors, and making sure everything ran smoothly behind the scenes. It was here that I realized how technology could bring people together, enhance experiences, and make a difference in ways I had not imagined. Every challenge, whether it was troubleshooting a glitch or designing a new system—felt like solving a puzzle, and I loved it. This journey not only sharpened my technical skills but also taught me the value of collaboration, patience, and creativity. What started as a way to serve my community became the spark for a lifelong love of coding, driving me to explore, learn, and create with technology.
